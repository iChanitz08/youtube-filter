# Privacy Policy — Optimism Chrome Extension

_Last updated: April 2026_

## What data we collect

When you use the 🚩 flag button to report a video, Optimism collects:
- The **video title** as it appears on YouTube
- The **channel name** as it appears on YouTube
- An optional **comment** you type explaining why the video is negative

No personal information (name, email, IP address, account details) is ever collected.

## How we use it

The video title, channel name, and comment are sent to a secure database and reviewed weekly by an AI pipeline. The purpose is to identify patterns in negative content and improve the keyword filter for all users.

## What we do not collect

- We do not collect your browsing history
- We do not collect your YouTube watch history or account information
- We do not collect any personally identifiable information
- We do not use cookies or tracking pixels

## Data storage

Reports are stored securely in Supabase. Only the developer has access to this data. It is never sold or shared with third parties.

## Your settings

Your filter preferences (enabled categories, custom keywords, blocked channels) are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in storage. This data never leaves your device.
